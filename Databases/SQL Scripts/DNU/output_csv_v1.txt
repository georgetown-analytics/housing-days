Monthly date format: yyyy-mm-01 (eg. 2013-10-01 )
Exact date format: yyyy-mm-dd (eg. 2013-10-07)



----QUERY----
select sales.*,
MVPSF.MedianValuePerSqft,
PHDV.PctOfHomesDecreasingInValues,
PHIV.PctOfHomesIncreasingInValues,
T.Turnover,
Zhvi.Zhvi,
FMACHPIWASDC.PriceIndex,
FreddieMac15yr.FreddieMac15yr,
FreddieMac5yrARM.FreddieMac5yrARM,
FreddieMacMIMI.mimi,
FreddieMacMIMI.mimiStatus
from sales
left join MVPSF on sales.Zip=MVPSF.RegionName and sales.IndexMonth=MVPSF.IndexMonth
left join PHDV on sales.Zip=PHDV.RegionName and sales.IndexMonth=PHDV.IndexMonth
left join PHIV on sales.Zip=PHIV.RegionName and sales.IndexMonth=PHIV.IndexMonth
left join T on sales.Zip=T.RegionName and sales.IndexMonth=T.IndexMonth
left join Zhvi on sales.Zip=Zhvi.RegionName and sales.IndexMonth=Zhvi.IndexMonth
left join FMACHPIWASDC on sales.IndexMonth=FMACHPIWASDC.IndexMonth
left join FreddieMac15yr on sales.IndexMonth=FreddieMac15yr.Month
left join FreddieMac5yrARM on sales.IndexMonth=FreddieMac5yrARM.MonthYear
left join FreddieMacMIMI on sales.IndexMonth=FreddieMacMIMI.MonthYear
